SwainOS Success Criteria and Build Phases

Phase 1: Foundation (2 weeks)
- Scope: Core schema, UI shell, manual debt entry, debt tracker.
- Success criteria: Track loan balance and view payment schedule.

Phase 2: Cash Flow (2 weeks)
- Scope: Cash projections, budget vs actual, deposits.
- Success criteria: 90-day cash flow forecast available.

Phase 3: Salesforce Integration (2 weeks)
- Scope: Booking pipeline and revenue visibility from Salesforce.
- Success criteria: Live booking data in system.

Phase 4: FX Engine (2 weeks)
- Scope: FX rates, exposure calculation, basic signals.
- Success criteria: First FX buy signal generated.

Phase 5: AI Layer (2 weeks)
- Scope: OpenAI integration, briefings, natural language queries.
- Success criteria: Natural language questions supported.

Phase 6: QuickBooks Integration (2 weeks)
- Scope: Full P&L sync and reporting.
- Success criteria: Full financial picture from QuickBooks.

Phase 7: Advanced FX (2+ weeks)
- Scope: News sentiment, macro signals, refined algorithm.
- Success criteria: FX signals incorporate news sentiment.
