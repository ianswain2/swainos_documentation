SwainOS Objectives

Operational objectives
- Unify Salesforce, QuickBooks, FX, and manual inputs through scheduled syncs.
- Maintain an internal data model decoupled from source schemas.
- Support one-way, read-only ingestion with audit logging and retries.

Financial objectives
- Forecast 30/60/90-day cash flow and runway.
- Track deposit liabilities versus available cash.
- Monitor debt schedules, DSCR, and covenant compliance.
- Provide budget vs. actual variance analysis.

FX objectives
- Measure current and projected FX exposure by currency and horizon.
- Generate actionable BUY/WAIT/HEDGE signals with reasoning.
- Track FX performance versus spot at payment date.

AI objectives
- Produce daily briefings and anomaly explanations.
- Enable natural language queries over core business data.
- Apply sentiment analysis to macro/FX news inputs.

Alignment note
