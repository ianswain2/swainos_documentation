SwainOS Goals

Primary goals
- Centralize all business data into a single source of truth.
- Deliver real-time cash flow visibility and forecasting.
- Optimize FX purchasing with AI-powered buy/hold signals.
- Enable natural language access to business intelligence.
- Track performance against budgets, loan covenants, and benchmarks.

Strategic outcomes
- Reduce manual spreadsheet workflows across finance and ops.
- Improve timing and profitability of FX decisions.
- Provide proactive, actionable insights instead of reactive reporting.

Alignment note
- Build progress is tracked in `SwianOS_Documentation/action-plan/action-log`.
